Charlie Anderson Red Sauce Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for Charlie Anderson's New York style pizza. I like this sauce specifically because it is a bit sweet, but keeps some of the tanginess of an uncooked sauce. With the addition of the incorporated basil and oregano, the sauce has another layer of aromatic.

Ingredients

  • 28 oz Bianco DiNapoli Organic Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  • 2 grams Salt
  • 12 grams Sugar
  • 2 grams Dried Oregano

Steps

  1. Add all of the dry herbs and seasonings to tomatoes
  2. Blend to desired consistency

Air Fryer Chicken Wings Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for air fryer chicken wings. They'll get crispy, almost as crispy as deep fried wings. They'll be way healthier than fried wings as well. Feel free to finish these wings in your hot sauce of choice.

Ingredients

  • 6 - 12 Pieces of Sectioned Chicken Wings
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil With High Smoke Point e.g. Avocado Oil
  • Blue Cheese or Ranch (If you're under 12 years old)
  • Optional: Finishing Seasoning of Choice e.g. Lemon Pepper
  • Optional: Finishing Sauce of Choice e.g. Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Rinse and pat dry the chicken wings
  2. Add a small amount of oil to the wings to act as a binder
  3. Season the wings with a little bit of salt and pepper.
  4. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F
  5. Place the chicken wings in the basket
  6. Cook for anywhere from 20 - 30 minutes, flipping halfway
